Study Planner 2024

A study plan is always good for the learning process of a student. It is also good for a student's future as a well planned stystematic plan to complete a task would not
only help in completing it, but to do it in a better and systematic way.In this study plan, to complete our module, we are going to sort the exercises of each chapters
and will be given a specific time when to do the task and complete it. 2 chapters are given for each day to complete a task.The study plan will span upto 2 weeks and
the 1st week will be given to the mandatory exercises and the bonus for the following week.The final days of each week will be given as a final touch day, as the
students are able to add or change their work on that day.
